Quantum Developer Day 2025

Quantum Developer Day is a dedicated event for quantum software development, bringing together leading developers, researchers, and enterprise technologists to showcase, build, and collaborate on real-world quantum computing software.

Date & Time

Sunday, Nov 2, 2025
1:00 PM – 6:00 PM CT

at The Chicago Quantum Summit

Venue

Convene at Willis Tower
233 S Wacker Dr
Chicago, IL 60606

Audience

Attendance at the in-person event is limited to enterprise leaders, technical decision-makers, quantum research teams, and advanced researchers. The live stream is open to everyone, including students and early-career professionals.
