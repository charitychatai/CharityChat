About Charity Chat

A simple concept: Turn all your AI searching into good.

How It Works

Every time you ask a question, Charity Chat displays carefully curated advertisements alongside your ChatGPT-powered response. 99% of all ad revenue goes directly to verified, high-impact charities—no middlemen, no overhead.

Your curiosity fuels lasting change. Simply by using Charity Chat for your daily questions, you're contributing to causes that matter—at no cost to you.

Our Partner Charities

We rotate donations monthly among the world's most effective charities, as rated by independent evaluators like GiveWell and Charity Navigator. Here are the top 10 organizations in our rotation:

Against Malaria Foundation

Prevents malaria by distributing long-lasting insecticide-treated nets to vulnerable populations in Africa.

GiveDirectly

Provides direct cash transfers to people living in extreme poverty, empowering them to make their own choices.

Helen Keller International

Delivers vitamin A supplementation to prevent childhood blindness and reduce mortality in developing countries.

Malaria Consortium

Provides seasonal malaria chemoprevention to children in sub-Saharan Africa, preventing millions of cases annually.

New Incentives

Uses conditional cash transfers to increase routine immunization rates in Nigeria, saving children's lives.

SCI Foundation

Treats and prevents parasitic worm infections in school-age children across Africa and Yemen.

The END Fund

Works to control and eliminate neglected tropical diseases that affect over 1 billion people worldwide.

Evidence Action

Scales proven interventions like safe water chlorination and deworming programs in developing countries.

Sightsavers

Prevents blindness and advocates for the rights of people with visual impairments in low-income countries.

Living Goods

Trains community health workers to deliver life-saving products and services door-to-door in East Africa.

Transparency & Accountability

We publish quarterly impact reports showing exactly how much revenue was generated and which charities received funding. All transactions are independently audited to ensure 99% of ad profit reaches the intended recipients.
